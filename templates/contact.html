Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! Please send us a message using the form above with the subject "Volunteer Opportunities" and tell us about your skills and interests.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, Hope Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How do you use donations?

90% of all donations go directly to our programs. The remaining 10% covers essential administrative and fundraising costs.

Can I visit your projects?

We organize volunteer trips and site visits periodically. Contact us to learn about upcoming opportunities.

Do you accept corporate partnerships?

Absolutely! We work with corporations for sponsorships, matching gifts, and employee volunteer programs. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

How can I stay updated?

Subscribe to our newsletter to receive regular updates about our work, impact stories, and upcoming events.
